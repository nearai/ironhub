### 1. Title

Explain official letters in simple language

### 2. Example prompt

"Explain this bank/government/insurance letter in simple language and tell me what action I need to take."

### 3. What the agent does

Reads the letter (uploaded as a PDF, image, or pasted text) and translates dense legal or bureaucratic language into plain English. It identifies the purpose of the letter, highlights any deadlines or amounts due, and produces a clear list of action steps — what you need to do, by when, and who to contact. It can also set a reminder for any upcoming deadlines.

### 4. Skills & tools used

- Document Reader tool — reads PDFs and images of letters, extracting the full text for analysis
- Simple Explanation skill — rewrites formal or legal language into clear, everyday terms and extracts the key facts, deadlines, and required actions
- Reminder tool — creates a deadline reminder for any response or action required by the letter
